Q. What is Synchronization in Selenium?
A. Process of matching the Selenium speed with application is called as  Synchronization.

There are five types of Wait, used in Selenium for synchronization.

1. Thread.Sleep
2. Implicit Wait
3. Fluent Wait
4. Explicit Wait, we can also call it as WebDriver wait.
5. Page Load Timeout.

They are applicable at different conditions while locating the web element.
